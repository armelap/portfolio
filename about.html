About Me

When people ask me how my passion for the marketing industry began, my response is a blend of ‘I never planned it’ and ‘It was meant to be.’ From a young age, I was captivated by the art of storytelling, the power of photography, and human psychology. I was always curious about the ‘why’ behind people’s actions and decisions. This curiosity naturally led me to explore creative ways of connecting with others.

My journey truly began with my first job, where I eagerly took on small marketing projects. I quickly discovered that marketing and content creation were not just tasks—they were sources of immense joy and adrenaline for me. The thrill of seeing people engage with an idea born from my creativity was unlike anything I had ever experienced. At that moment, everything clicked, and I realized that this was my true calling.

This passion has since blossomed into a series of enriching experiences in the marketing field, shaping who I am today. I am Armela, but my friends and family call me ‘Mela,’ which means ‘apple’ in Italian.
