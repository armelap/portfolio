Leap ‘n Love campaign


Background: In February 2024, Key Auto Group (KAG) launched the “Love & Leap” campaign, merging the excitement of Leap Year with the romance of Valentine’s Day to create a memorable car-buying experience.

Goal: Move unsold aged pre-owned units, increase sales by 10%, and boost online visibility.

Promotion: $2900 OFF 29 units, Enter to win: with every purchase, Giveaway campaign.


My role: Developed the campaign strategy, managed social media ads, created vehicle reels, coordinated promotional efforts, and tracked performance.


Results: Sales increased by 20%, gained 160 new Instagram followers.
